Using update household

🎓 Watch Tutorial

Update your Netflix household to include this device permanently

  1. From Netflix home screen, press back button to open menu
  2. Navigate to 'Get Help' > 'Manage Netflix Household'
  3. Select 'Update Netflix Household'
  4. Choose 'Send Email' or 'Send Text' for verification
  5. Check your email/text for verification link
  6. Click 'Yes, This Was Me' in email or tap link in text
  7. Select 'Confirm Update' to complete the process
